Introduction

Welcome to WM Appliances. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information Collection and Use

We collect information from you when you register on our site, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or fill out a form. When ordering or registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, or credit card information.

Privacy Policy: SMS Communications Consent

By submitting your mobile phone number to WM APPLIANCES, you are expressly granting us permission to send you SMS messages related to your order status, exclusive offers, and promotional information that we believe may be of interest to you. Our aim is to keep you well-informed and provide you with personalized updates and marketing content that enhances your experience with our services and products.

Opting Out of SMS Communications

We respect your preferences and control over your personal information. Should you decide at any time that you no longer wish to receive these SMS messages from us, you have the right to opt-out. To stop receiving SMS communications, simply reply to any of our messages with the word "STOP". Upon receiving your opt-out request, we will promptly remove your number from our SMS communications list and you will not receive further messages from us in this format. It's important to note that opting out of SMS communications does not affect other forms of communication you may have consented to, such as email updates. If you have any questions or need assistance with managing your communication preferences, please contact our support team at "support@wmappliances.net". Thank you for being a valued part of the WM APPLIANCES community. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience with our brand.

Information Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your web browser (if you allow) that enables the site's or service provider's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Third Party Links

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third-party products or services on our website. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies.

Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our privacy policy at any time, and any changes will be posted on this page.
