Privacy Policy for GPT MatchMaze App

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information in connection with the GPT Matchmaking App.

Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you input your interests, hobbies, and preferences into our conversational UI. This information is used to match you with other users.

Use of Information

The information you provide is processed and structured by our backend server for matching purposes. We use a matching algorithm to find users with similar profiles.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers and is protected against unauthorized access and use. We implement strict security protocols and adhere to privacy laws.

User Consent and Control

You have control over the information you share. You can opt in or out of being contacted and can update your privacy settings at any time.

Communication with Matches

Upon mutual acceptance, a channel of communication is established within the platform. Your contact details are only shared with your consent.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes and encouraged to review the policy periodically.
